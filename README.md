# Superstore-Retail-Performance-Analyzer
Developed an interactive Retail Performance Analytics Dashboard using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and regional business trends. The dashboard enables business users to monitor KPIs, identify high-performing products and regions, and support data-driven business decisions.

**Business Problem**

Retail organizations generate thousands of sales transactions across different products, customers, and regions. Without proper analysis, identifying profitable products, underperforming locations, and customer purchasing patterns becomes challenging.
This dashboard enables business users to:

-Monitor overall sales and profitability
-Identify high-performing states and product categories
-Compare regional business performance
-Track monthly sales and profit trends
-Analyze customer purchasing preferences
-Support data-driven strategic decisions
